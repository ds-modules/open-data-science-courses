# edX Courses

UC Berkeley offers data science courses on the [edX platform](https://www.edx.org). This page gives an overview of what these courses are and how they are structured.

## What These Courses Are

Our edX data science courses bring the Berkeley curriculum to learners worldwide. You can take them for **free** to learn at your own pace, or pay for a **verified certificate** when you want a credential. Content is the same either way; payment is only for the certificate.

**How to access:** Go to [edX.org](https://www.edx.org), search for the course name or "UC Berkeley," and enroll. Use the links in the sidebar to open each course’s overview and part pages.

## How Each Course Is Structured

Each course is divided into **three parts**:

- **Part 1** — Foundations: core concepts, setup, and the basics for that course.
- **Part 2** — Core content: main topics, methods, and applications.
- **Part 3** — Advanced topics and synthesis: deeper material and bringing everything together.

What is covered in each part depends on the course. Choose a course in the sidebar (C88C, 88B, 88E) to see its overview and Part 1, Part 2, and Part 3 pages.
