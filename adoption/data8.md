# Data 8

Materials and adoption information for **Data 8**.

**Course Overview:** Data 8: Foundations of Data Science provides a comprehensive introduction to data science principles and practices. The course emphasizes computational thinking, statistical reasoning, and data analysis skills using Python. Students explore topics including data collection and cleaning, exploratory data analysis, hypothesis testing, and predictive modeling. Through hands-on projects and real-world applications, students develop the ability to extract insights from data and make data-driven decisions. This course is designed for students from diverse backgrounds and serves as a gateway to advanced data science and statistics courses.
