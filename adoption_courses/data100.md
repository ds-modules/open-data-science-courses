# Data 100

Materials and adoption information for **Data 100**.

**Course Overview:** Data 100: Principles and Techniques of Data Science builds upon foundational data science concepts to explore advanced topics in machine learning, statistical modeling, and big data processing. Students learn to apply sophisticated analytical techniques to large-scale datasets, including supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms, model validation, and scalable data processing with tools like SQL and distributed computing frameworks. The course emphasizes practical applications, ethical considerations in data science, and the development of end-to-end data science workflows. This advanced course prepares students for research and industry roles in data science and related fields.
