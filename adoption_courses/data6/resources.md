# Additional Resources

This section provides links to additional resources for teaching Data 6 and related courses.

## Course Materials

- [Data 6 Course Website](https://data6.org) - Syllabi and assignments for semesters at UC Berkeley
- [Data 6 Course Notes](https://data6.org/notes/) - Online lecture notes
- [Public Student Materials](https://github.com/dubois-ctds/data-6-materials-student) - Jupyter notebooks for labs, homeworks, and projects
- [Data 6 GitHub Organization](https://github.com/data-6-berkeley)

## Foundational Texts

We strongly recommend supplementing Data 6 materials with these foundational texts:

- **Data 8**: [_Computational and Inferential Thinking: The Foundations of Data Science_](https://inferentialthinking.com/), 2nd Edition, by Ani Adhikari, John DeNero, David Wagner
- **CS 61A**: [_Composing Programs_](https://www.composingprograms.com/), by John DeNero
- **Stat 20**: [Course Notes](https://stat20.berkeley.edu/spring-2026/notes.htmls) by Andrew Bray
