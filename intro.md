# Introduction

Welcome to **Open Data Science Courses** — a centralized repository of adoptable data science courses from UC Berkeley, including Canvas shells, autograders, and links to our edX offerings.

## What you'll find here

- **Adoption Pages** — Guides and materials for adopting Data 6 and Data 8 at your institution.
- **edX Courses** — Information and links for our edX offerings (C88C and 88B).
