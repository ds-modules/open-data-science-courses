# Introduction

Welcome to **Open Data Science Courses** — a centralized repository of adoptable data science courses from UC Berkeley, including Canvas shells, autograders, and links to our edX offerings.

## What you'll find here

- **Adoption Pages** — Guides and materials for adopting Data 6 and Data 8 <!--, and Data 100 -->at your institution. These courses provide foundational to advanced data science education, covering programming, statistics, machine learning, and real-world applications.
- **edX Courses** — Information and links for our edX offerings (C88C, 88B, and 88E), which focus on computational thinking and data structures essential for data science practitioners.

This repository serves as a comprehensive resource for educators looking to implement or adapt UC Berkeley's data science curriculum, featuring course materials, infrastructure guides, and best practices for teaching data science at scale.
